# Farmer Dashboard - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Your New Dashboard! 🎉

Your farmer dashboard has been completely redesigned to make it easier to manage your farm. Here's everything you need to know.

## What's New?

### 🎯 One Dashboard, All Features
Instead of clicking through multiple menus, everything is now on one page organized into clear categories.

### 🎨 Color-Coded Categories
Each category has its own color to help you find what you need faster:
- 🟣 **Purple** = AI Intelligence
- 🟢 **Green** = Production & Supply
- 🔵 **Blue** = Orders & Logistics
- 🟠 **Orange** = Payments & Finance
- 🌸 **Pink** = Tenders & Bidding
- 🟡 **Yellow** = Trust & Reputation
- 🔴 **Red** = Security & Compliance

### 📱 Works Great on Mobile
The new design works perfectly on your phone or tablet.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Open Your Dashboard
Navigate to your farmer dashboard. You'll see a welcome screen with all feature categories.

### Step 2: Find Your Feature
Scroll through the categories to find what you need. Each feature has:
- **Icon** - Visual identifier
- **Name** - What it's called
- **Description** - What it does

### Step 3: Click to Open
Click any feature to open it in a full-page view.

### Step 4: Go Back
Click the "Back to Dashboard" button to return to the main view.

## Feature Categories Explained

### 🤖 AI Intelligence
Smart insights powered by artificial intelligence:
- **Farm Insights**: Check weather, soil health, and pest detection
- **Agri Intelligence**: Get AI-powered farming recommendations
- **Market Intelligence**: See price trends and market forecasts
- **Behavioral Insights**: Understand buyer behavior

**When to use**: Planning your farming activities, checking market conditions

### 📦 Production & Supply
Manage your products and inventory:
- **Product Hub**: Add, edit, and manage all your products
- **Smart Inventory**: Track stock levels and availability
- **AI Quality Scan**: Scan crops to check quality instantly
- **Crop Management**: Plan and track your crop cycles

**When to use**: Managing what you grow and sell

### 🚚 Orders & Logistics
Track orders and manage deliveries:
- **Order Control**: View and manage all your orders
- **Logistics Manager**: Track deliveries and shipments
- **Auto-Sell Rules**: Set up automated selling rules

**When to use**: Handling customer orders and deliveries

### 💰 Payments & Finance
Financial management and payments:
- **AgriPay Center**: Process payments and transactions
- **Escrow Hub**: Secure transactions with escrow protection
- **Financial Hub**: View revenue and financial analytics
- **Price Protection**: Lock in prices to protect your income

**When to use**: Managing money and finances

### ⚖️ Tenders & Bidding
Participate in tenders and bulk orders:
- **Tender Bids Hub**: Browse and bid on tenders
- **My Tenders**: Track your active tender participations
- **Bulk Aggregation**: Join with other farmers for group selling

**When to use**: Finding bulk buyers and tender opportunities

### 🏆 Trust & Reputation
Build your reputation and trust:
- **Trust Identity**: View your reputation score and profile
- **Reputation Hub**: Manage reviews and ratings
- **Blockchain Trace**: Verify product authenticity on blockchain

**When to use**: Building credibility with buyers

### 🛡️ Security & Compliance
Stay secure and compliant:
- **Security Dashboard**: Monitor security and threats
- **Compliance Tracking**: Track regulatory compliance
- **Export Audit**: Manage export documentation

**When to use**: Ensuring security and meeting regulations

## Common Tasks

3. Click "Product Hub"
4. Click "Add Product" button
5. Fill in product details
6. Save

### Checking Orders
1. Open Dashboard
2. Find "Orders & Logistics" (blue card)
3. Click "Order Control"
4. View all your orders

### Viewing Finances
1. Open Dashboard
2. Find "Payments & Finance" (orange card)
3. Click "Financial Hub"
4. See revenue and analytics

### Scanning Crop Quality
1. Open Dashboard
2. Find "Production & Supply" (green card)
