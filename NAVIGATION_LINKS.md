## 📱 How to Access New Features

### For Farmers:
1. Login to farmer account
2. Look at the left sidebar navigation
3. Click on:
   - **"My Orders"** to see all orders
   - **"Wallet"** to manage funds
   - **"Analytics"** to view performance metrics

### For Buyers:
1. Login to buyer account
2. Look at the left sidebar navigation
3. Click on:
   - **"Wallet"** to add funds
   - **"My Orders"** to track purchases
   - **"Analytics"** to view spending insights

### For FPOs:
1. Login to FPO account
2. Look at the left sidebar navigation
3. Click on:
   - **"My Orders"** to manage bulk orders
   - **"Wallet"** to view commission
   - **"Analytics"** to see FPO performance
