## 🔍 Damage Detection Features

### Detects
- **Bruises**: Brown/yellow spots from impact
- **Rot**: Dark/black spots from decay
- **Discoloration**: Uneven coloring
- **Texture Issues**: Rough vs smooth

### Measures
- **Good Area %**: Healthy portions
- **Damaged Area %**: Defective portions
- **Spot Count**: Number of damage spots
- **Severity**: Moderate or severe

### Grades
- **Excellent**: No damage (< 10%)
- **Good**: Minor damage (10-25%)
- **Fair**: Moderate damage (25-50%)
- **Poor**: Significant damage (> 50%)
